# Wellness Connect Brand Guidelines

## Brand Source Of Truth

**Brand name:** Wellness Connect  
**Tagline:** Mind. Body. Better Together.  
**Category:** Wellness platform for counselling, fitness, appointments, progress tracking, and support.

Use **Wellness Connect** in all user-facing copy, documentation, code examples, metadata, and product references. Do not use previous working names.

## Brand Personality

- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Motivating
- Professional
- Human
- Premium
- Supportive

## Voice Principles

**Clear:** Use direct, plain language. Avoid clinical jargon unless the user needs it.

**Supportive:** Make the user feel guided, not judged.

**Responsible:** Avoid guaranteeing mental health, fitness, or medical outcomes.

**Actionable:** Prefer next steps over vague encouragement.

**Respectful:** Treat counselling, training, and personal progress as sensitive user contexts.

## Product Language

| Use | Avoid |
| --- | --- |
| Counselling | Therapy claims without clinical context |
| Personal training | Gym only |
| Appointments | Meetings |
| Progress | Performance ranking |
| Support | Help desk only |
| Member | Patient, unless a clinical workflow requires it |
| Expert | Coach only, unless the role is specific |

## Visual Principles

- Use calm surfaces and enough whitespace for sensitive wellness moments.
- Use primary color for important action and active state, not decoration.
- Use green and success language carefully; completion is positive, but progress should not shame users.
- Keep cards structured and scannable, especially for appointments, support tickets, and progress summaries.
- Prefer human, practical content over decorative wellness cliches.

## Content Safety

- Do not promise diagnosis, cure, or guaranteed transformation.
- Use crisis and emergency support language only when approved by product and legal stakeholders.
- Make cancellation, rescheduling, and support pathways easy to find.
- Treat personal notes, mood, and progress data as sensitive information.
