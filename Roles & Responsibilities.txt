## 👔 Add These Core Roles

### 1) CA / Finance Lead

**What they’ll handle**

* Business structure & registrations (GST, PAN/TAN)
* **GST strategy** (services vs products, place of supply, mixed supplies)
* **Subscriptions & invoicing** (SaaS-style billing, credits, refunds)
* **Payroll & TDS** (for trainers, counsellors—employee vs contractor)
* **Financial reporting** (P&L, cash flow, unit economics)
* **Compliance calendar** (returns, filings)

**Early decisions with CA**

* Pvt Ltd vs LLP
* Pricing + tax inclusion/exclusion
* Revenue recognition (sessions vs packages)

---

### 2) Legal Advisor

**What they’ll handle**

* **Company formation docs** & shareholder agreements
* **User-facing documents**:

  * Terms of Service
  * Privacy Policy (DPDP Act, India)
  * Consent forms (especially for counselling)
  * Refund & Cancellation policy
* **Professional agreements**:

  * Counsellor contracts (ethics, confidentiality)
  * Trainer agreements
  * Non-solicitation (prevent taking clients offline)
* **Risk & liability**

  * Medical disclaimers (you’re not providing emergency care)
  * Indemnities
  * Data protection clauses
* **IP & brand**

  * Trademark “WellRise”
  * Content ownership

## ⚠️ Special Considerations for Your App

### 🧠 Counselling (High sensitivity)

* Explicit **informed consent**
* Data privacy (mental health = sensitive personal data)
* Session confidentiality & storage policy
* Crisis handling disclaimer (not for emergencies)

### 🏋️ Fitness

* Injury disclaimer
* Health declaration before programs
* Trainer liability clauses

### 💳 Payments

* Razorpay/Stripe terms alignment with your policies
* Refund SLAs clearly defined

### 🛍️ Store (if added)

* Product liability
* Returns policy
* Vendor agreements
